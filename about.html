What is Spotless?

Spotless examines the slowly fading presence of community spaces, exploring how this gradual loss disrupts our sense of connection and belonging. The goal for this project is to create an educational and communal experience for our audience, inspiring conversation about how we can preserve third spaces while adapting them to fit into the needs of our changing world.
